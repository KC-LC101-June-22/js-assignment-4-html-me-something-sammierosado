Dark Cloud

This is one of the first games I played by myself. Before this game I would watch my Dad play all the games and he'd let me play sometimes when he got stuck on something.

Jak and Daxter

I absolutely adored this game. It made me a lifelong fan of Naughty Dog who also created Crash Bandicoot and Last of Us.

Super Smash Bros. Melee

I remember playing this game on my cousins Gamecube. This was the first time I was fully addicted to a video game.

Mario Superstar Baseball

I am very fond of Mario Superstar Baseball because I used to play this with my whole family- even my mom who rarely played games with us.

Golden Sun

The story of this game was unforgettable, I have wanted to replay it for years.

Harvest Moon: Another Wonderful Life

This was my first Harvest Moon game. I love how relaxing and calming this game makes me feel.

Sonic Adventure 2: Battle

I am still a hardcore Sonic the hedgehog fan to this day because of this game. Despite Sonic being cringe nowadays.

The Sims

I would play this game during my summer. I have vivid memories of my laptop overheating because I would play it for hours on end.

Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace

One of the earliest games I remember playing.

Pokemon Yellow

This is one of the first games I played by myself.
